**Research Report: Agentic AI in 2026**

**Introduction**

Agentic AI is a rapidly evolving field that is transforming the way businesses operate. In 2026, agentic AI is expected to play a crucial role in driving innovation, productivity, and efficiency across various industries. This research report provides an overview of the key trends, insights, and applications of agentic AI in 2026.

**Key Concepts**

Agentic AI is a type of artificial intelligence that enables agents to perform tasks autonomously, making decisions and taking actions based on their environment and goals. The following key concepts are essential to understanding agentic AI:

1. **Agentic AI Design Patterns**: Seven must-know design patterns (ReAct, Reflection, Tool Use, Planning, Multi-Agent Collaboration, Sequential Workflows, and Human-in-the-Loop) form the architectural vocabulary for agent development.
2. **Long-term Memory**: Three types of long-term memory (episodic, semantic, and procedural) enable agents to learn and improve over time.
3. **Small Language Models**: Small language models are cost-effective alternatives to massive LLMs for many agent tasks.
4. **FinOps for AI Agents**: Cost optimization is a core architecture for agentic AI systems.
5. **Agentic AI in Action**: Agentic AI is being used in various industries, including cloud cost optimization, security incident response, and financial monitoring.

**Important Insights**

This section highlights the key insights and implications of agentic AI in 2026:

1. **Agentic AI will reshape engineering workflows**: Agentic AI will run first drafts of the SDLC, leaving humans to steer, review, and think bigger.
2. **Agentic AI will drive global manufacturing**: Agentic AI will impact operational workflows by acting as a digital co-worker that reasons through problems and coordinates across systems end-to-end.
3. **Agentic AI governance is critical**: Strong AI governance allows organizations to evaluate the performance of AI agents and ensure that they are aligned with business objectives.
4. **Agentic AI will require new skills**: Technology leaders will need to develop new skills to orchestrate and manage agentic AI systems.
5. **Agentic AI will lead to better processes and outcomes**: Agentic AI will enable better processes, better outcomes, and better operational performance.
